A new HAT for rPI that enables audio processing with audio input, knobs and buttons. What drew me in was the article which referenced a number of audio software, including:
* Volumio
* RuneAudio
* PureData
* SuperCollider
* Audacity
* Sonic Pi
* LV2 Plugins

More investigation required.
